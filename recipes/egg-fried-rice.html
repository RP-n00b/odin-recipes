Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg Fried Rice is a simple and quick recipe which uses leftover rice to make a delicous dish.

Ingredients

  • Leftover cold rice, ideally jasmine rice
  • Green Onions or Spring Onions
  • Regular Soy Sauce
  • Toasted Sesame Oil
  • Beaten Eggs
  • Neutral Oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil.
  2. Fry beaten eggs.
  3. Toss in rice.
  4. Toss in green onions.
  5. Add seasoning.
  6. Serve.
